About Cyber_Jay

Starting his journey at Apam Senior High School, he excelled in academics and leadership, representing his school in national and international competitions like Robofest and WRO. As the leader of the Robotics Club, he led his team to significant success. His other projects include a PC download manager and a Telegram bot suite with secure payment integration. In addition to these, Jeffrey(Cyber Jay) is building, a tech initiative that blends his skills and passion for creating next-gen tools. He is also developing a TikTok bot to automate content curation, making processes more efficient for creators. With dreams of becoming a pilot, he blends his love for aviation with his deep interest in technology, aiming to make an impact as a world-class technologist and future pilot.
